About Ashland

Ashland is a city in southern Oregon, known for its Shakespeare Festival and natural beauty.

City Statistics

  • Population: 21,360 (2020 estimate)
  • Incorporated: October 13, 1874
  • Region: Southern Oregon
  • Classification: Small Urban/Suburban
  • Average Income: Slightly above state average

Notable Attractions

  1. Oregon Shakespeare Festival
  2. Lithia Park
  3. ScienceWorks Hands-On Museum
