About Eugene

Eugene is a city in Oregon's Willamette Valley, known for its natural beauty, outdoor recreation, and the University of Oregon.

City Statistics

  • Population: 176,654 (2020 estimate)
  • Incorporated: October 17, 1862
  • Region: Willamette Valley
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: Slightly below state average

Notable Attractions

  1. University of Oregon
  2. Alton Baker Park
  3. Mount Pisgah Arboretum

Eugene is nicknamed "Track Town, USA" due to its long history of track and field athletics.
